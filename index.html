Lonnies Western Room

Voted Nashville’s #1 Local Karaoke Bar

Voted #10 College Bar in America

Open Thursday, Friday, and Saturday from 6PM-3AM

Now serving pay-by-the-slice pizza!

Lonnie’s Western Room has provided the best in Karaoke entertainment since 1989.

You will find an astounding song selection to choose from that will satisfy every singer’s appetite. From country, rock, hip-hop, and oldies the music never stops, and neither do the good times!

Lonnie’s Western Room employs the best local singers, bartenders, and waitresses. With easy parking & food close by, Lonnie’s is the clear choice for your night of entertainment. If you're in Nashville, TN or close by, come on out and join us!

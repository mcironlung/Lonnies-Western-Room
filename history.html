History of Lonnies Western Room

Back in the 60’s and 70’s, this location was called the “VooDoo Room” which was a piano bar. Local TV and radio personality Teddy Bart once played the piano there when he first came to Nashville.The current TV hit “Nashville Star” began at Lonnie’s Western Room.

Lonnie’s has been featured in Stuff Magazine, Esquire and voted number One Karaoke Bar in Nashville by the Nashville Scene!
